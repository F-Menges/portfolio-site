Cookie Policy

Last updated: 5/2/2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Felix Menges uses cookies and similar technologies on my portfolio website, felixmenges.me.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They help improve user experience by remembering preferences and providing anonymized tracking data to third-party applications like Google Analytics.

How I Use Cookies

I use cookies solely for analytics purposes. Specifically:

  • Google Analytics: I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the Website. This helps me improve the content and performance of the site. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.

Google Analytics may set the following types of cookies:

  • _ga
  • _gid
  • _gat

For more information about how Google Analytics uses cookies, you can visit: https://developers.google.com/analytics/devguides/collection/analyticsjs/cookie-usage

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
