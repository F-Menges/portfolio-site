About Felix

My name is Felix Menges, and I am an undergraduate student at the University of Iowa, studying Business Analytics and Information Systems, with an anticipated graduation date of December 2025. I have been a top student at the University of Iowa, making it onto the President’s List every semester since my freshman year and into the Tippie Business Honor Society — an exclusive organization for the top 10% of Tippie students.

I work part-time for the University of Iowa College of Liberal Arts & Sciences Marketing and Communications team. When I started this role, I was involved in the College's website migration to Drupal 9 to help update the College’s brand image while upgrading the site before support for Drupal 7 ended. Now that web migration is complete, I help maintain various department websites under the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences domain by creating web strategies to optimize user experiences.

After graduation, I am considering getting a Master's in Urban Planning to pursue a career as a transportation planner. Please view my resume below to learn more about my skills and experience.
